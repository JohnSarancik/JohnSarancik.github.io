Mixed Reality

CSMA 113


Professor Ivaylo Getov


This was the project I was most proud of in the course. It challenged my knowledge of C# and showed me what cool things could be made in virtual reality, augmented reality, and mixed reality. This project is an app that I developed to take remote controlled cars to another level. Without the hassle of a clunky controller, I wanted an app where people could enjoy driving toy cars from the convenience of their own device. The app allows you to spawn in a lamborghini and drive it along the plane generated with in-game physics. There are also power ups you can collect to boost your car. The app gives room for adding additional car assets and more powerups.
